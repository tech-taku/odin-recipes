Air Frier Potatoes Recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces baby gold potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon melted unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Directions

Step 1

Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) according to manufacturerâ€™s instructions

Step2

Place potatoes in a microwave safe bowl. Cook for 5 minutes. Transfer potatoes to a baking sheet.

Step 3

Using a small glass or shot glass, flatten potatoes. The thinner you smash, the crispier they will be. They do need to stay in one piece.

Step 4

Brush melted butter over the tops of smashed potatoes. Season with salt, garlic powder, and pepper.

Step 5

Place smashed potatoes in the basket of the air fryer, making sure to not overcrowd. Cook for 10 minutes. Garnish with parsley.

Enjoy!
